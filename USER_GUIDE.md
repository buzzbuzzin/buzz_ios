## Getting Started

### First Time Setup

1. Download and open the Buzz app
2. You'll see the welcome screen with authentication options
3. Choose how you want to sign in:
   - Email & Password
   - Phone Number
   - Google Account
   - Apple Account

### Creating Your Account

#### For Pilots:
1. Tap "Sign Up"
2. Select "I am a Pilot"
3. Choose your unique call sign (e.g., "MAVERICK", "EAGLE1")
4. Enter your credentials
5. Complete registration

#### For Customers:
1. Tap "Sign Up"
2. Select "I am a Customer"
3. Enter your credentials
4. Complete registration

## Pilot User Guide

### Dashboard Overview

After logging in as a pilot, you'll see four main tabs:

1. **Jobs** - Browse available work
2. **My Flights** - Track your bookings
3. **Rankings** - See your position
4. **Profile** - Manage your account

### 1. Jobs Tab - Finding Work

**List View:**
- Scroll through available drone service bookings
- Each card shows:
  - Location name
  - Job description
  - Payment amount üí∞
  - Estimated flight hours ‚è±Ô∏è
  - When it was posted

**Map View:**
- Tap the map icon in the top right
- See all bookings plotted on a map
- Red markers show available jobs
- Tap a marker to see quick details
- Tap the arrow to view full details

**Accepting a Job:**
1. Tap on any booking to see details
2. Review:
   - Exact location on map
   - Full description
   - Payment amount
   - Estimated duration
   - Status
3. Tap "Accept Booking"
4. Confirm your acceptance
5. Job moves to your "My Flights"

### 2. My Flights Tab - Managing Work

**Active Section:**
- Shows jobs you've accepted
- Tap to view details
- Mark as completed when done

**Completed Section:**
- Historical record of finished jobs
- View your past work

**Completing a Job:**
1. Tap on an accepted booking
2. After completing the work, tap "Mark as Completed"
3. Confirm completion
4. Your flight hours automatically update
5. Your tier may increase!

### 3. Rankings Tab - Your Progress

**Your Stats:**
- Current tier (0-10)
- Tier name (Novice ‚Üí Grand Master)
- Total flight hours
- Number of completed bookings

**Tier System:**
- **Tier 0 - Novice**: 0-10 hours
- **Tier 1 - Apprentice**: 10-25 hours
- **Tier 2 - Intermediate**: 25-50 hours
- **Tier 3 - Skilled**: 50-100 hours
- **Tier 4 - Advanced**: 100-200 hours
- **Tier 5 - Expert**: 200-350 hours
- **Tier 6 - Master**: 350-550 hours
- **Tier 7 - Elite**: 550-800 hours
- **Tier 8 - Legend**: 800-1100 hours
- **Tier 9 - Supreme**: 1100-1500 hours
- **Tier 10 - Grand Master**: 1500+ hours

**Leaderboard:**
- See top pilots ranked by flight hours
- Compare your stats with others
- Pull down to refresh rankings

### 4. Profile Tab - Your Account

**View Your Profile:**
- See your call sign
- View your tier and stats
- Check contact information

**Manage Licenses:**
1. Tap "Manage Licenses"
2. Tap + to upload a new license
3. Choose to:
   - Take a photo with camera
   - Select from photo library
   - Choose a PDF file
4. Your license is securely stored
5. View all uploaded licenses
6. Swipe to delete if needed

**Edit Profile:**
1. Tap "Edit Profile"
2. Update your information:
   - Call sign
   - Email
   - Phone number
3. Tap "Save Changes"

**Sign Out:**
- Tap "Sign Out"
- Confirm to log out

## Customer User Guide

### Dashboard Overview

After logging in as a customer, you'll see two main tabs:

1. **Bookings** - Create and manage bookings
2. **Profile** - Manage your account

### 1. Bookings Tab - Requesting Services

**Creating a Booking:**
1. Tap the + button in the top right
2. **Select Location:**
   - Tap anywhere on the map
   - The pin shows your selected location
   - Location name auto-populates
3. **Add Details:**
   - Description: What do you need?
   - Payment Amount: How much you'll pay
   - Estimated Hours: Expected job duration
4. Tap "Create Booking"
5. Your booking is now visible to pilots!

**Viewing Your Bookings:**
- All your bookings appear in a list
- Each card shows:
  - Location
  - Description
  - Payment amount
  - Status (Available, Accepted, Completed)
  - Creation date

**Booking Status:**
- üü¢ **Available**: Waiting for a pilot
- üîµ **Accepted**: Pilot has taken the job
- ‚ö™ **Completed**: Job finished
- üî¥ **Cancelled**: Booking cancelled

**Managing Bookings:**
1. Tap any booking to view details
2. See the location on a map
3. View which pilot accepted (if accepted)
4. Cancel if needed (while available)

### 2. Profile Tab - Your Account

**View Your Profile:**
- See your account information
- View contact details

**Edit Profile:**
1. Tap "Edit Profile"
2. Update your information
3. Save changes

**Sign Out:**
- Tap "Sign Out" to log out

## Troubleshooting

### Can't Sign In?
- Check your email/password
- Verify internet connection
- Try "Forgot Password" if available

### Booking Not Appearing?
- Pull down to refresh
- Check your internet connection
- Verify booking was created

### Map Not Loading?
- Check location permissions
- Grant access in Settings
- Restart the app

### File Upload Failed?
- Check file size (keep under 10MB)
- Verify internet connection
- Try a different file

### Tier Not Updating?
- Flight hours update when job completed
- Pull down to refresh stats
- Check "My Flights" for completed jobs
